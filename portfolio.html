How Women in Tech Talk About Themselves on Twitter

In August, #WITBragDay was trending on Twitter for about two days in the week after the release of the Google diversity memo. It was essentially supposed to be a way for women in tech to talk about cool things they had done. I scraped the tweets and did an analysis of the ways in which women were "bragging" about themselves. The project ended up expanding into a presentation that I gave at the eUSR conference in November 2017, a conference for undergraduates in statistics.
